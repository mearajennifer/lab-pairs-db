Create Lab Pairs

Create pairs for a cohort for Hackbright lab exercises with Baloonicorn's handy dandy matching machine. The algorithm looks for negative student interactions and makes sure not to pair those students together. Then it looks at how many times a student has been paired with all other potential pairs, and chooses a partner they've been matched with the least number of times. Thus, ensuring students can pair with every cohortmate at least once.
